### Spawning
**Chickzilla** is summoned by using an Omega Egg on a normal Chicken.

### Stats
- HP - 1000
- Defense - 0
- Speed - 0.25
- Damage - 0

### Special Ability
- When damaged, Chickzilla will reflect half of the damage back to the attacker.

### Loot
- Braided Feather - 10%
