### Spawning
When a Superior Dragon fails to spawn when summoning an Ender Dragon, one of the following variants is randomly picked:
- Unstable Dragon
- Strong Dragon
- Holy Dragon
- Protector Dragon
- Young Dragon
- Old Dragon
- Wise Dragon

### Stats & Special Abilities
**Unstable Dragon**
- HP - 200
- Defense - 0
- Speed - 0.7
- Regularly summons lightning on all mobs in a 64-block radius.

**Strong Dragon**
- HP - 200
- Defense - 0
- Speed - 0.7
- Deals more damage.

**Holy Dragon**
- HP - 200
- Defense - 0
- Speed - 0.7
- Permanent Regeneration I

**Protector Dragon**
- HP - 200
- Defense - 12.5
- Speed - 0.7

**Young Dragon**
- HP - 200
- Defense - 0
- Speed - 0.7
- When hit, has a 10% chance to teleport up to 32 blocks away.

**Old Dragon**
- HP - 400
- Defense - 0
- Speed - 0.7

**Wise Dragon**
- HP - 200
- Defense - 0
- Speed - 0.7

### Loot
- Remnant of the Superior Dragon - 2%
