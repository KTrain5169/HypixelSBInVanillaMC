### Spawning
**Necron** has a 25% chance to be selected when spawning a Wither.  He has an equal weight to all three other Wither variants.

### Stats
- HP - 300
- Defense - 0
- Speed - 0.6
- Damage - 16

### Loot
- Nether Star - 1x (unaffected by Looting)
- Necron's Handle - 5%
- Necron's Secrets - 1%
